Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe?

Yes! We use Roblox's official affiliate commission system. You're buying items the normal way - just through our game instead of the catalog directly.

Why is there a 7-day wait?

Roblox holds affiliate commissions for 7 days before releasing them. We pass this same waiting period to you.

How do I withdraw?

Once your balance reaches 100 Robux and the 7-day pending period is over, you can request a withdrawal. We'll send Robux directly to your account via group payout or by purchasing your gamepass.

Does this work for all items?

Most catalog items work, including accessories, clothing, and game passes. However, bundles (like Headless Horseman) typically don't work with the affiliate system.

Payout Timeline
